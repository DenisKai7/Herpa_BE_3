# Persona Output Rules

## Umum
Fast-medium: direct, everyday Indonesian, 5-10 items, no IUPAC, no mechanisms, no clinical claims. Nutrients separated from active phytochemicals.

## Pelajar
Adds definition/class explanation and simple learning summary.

## Peneliti
May show scientific name, simplisia, PubChem CID, molecular formula, IUPAC when relevant.

## Tenaga Medis
Shows pharmacologically relevant components and grounded safety caveats only. No fabricated dose, diagnosis, or clinical certainty.
