# Persona Formatter

`app/services/ai/persona_formatter.py` formats deterministic direct answers.

## Umum
Short direct list. No IUPAC/formula/targets. Nutrients separated from active phytochemicals.

## Pelajar
Adds class/group explanation and simple definition.

## Peneliti
May include class, PubChem CID, formula, and IUPAC when relevant.

## Tenaga Medis
Lists pharmacologically relevant components and safety caveat. No fabricated dose or interaction.
